A free and open source [local-first](https://lofi.so/) podcast application 
that embraces open technologies such as RSS and the 
[Podcast Index](https://podcastindex.org/).

By utilising TanStack DB, data is fetched in the background and updated with 
live data while showing cached results with an eventual plan to sync between
clients.

Future plans to add desktop and mobile clients.
